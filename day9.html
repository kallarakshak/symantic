TATA Consultancy

Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) is a leading provider of IT services, consulting and business solutions, and outsourcing. Since the founding of the firm in 1968, TCS has partnered with some of the world’s largest businesses. Over the past 50 years, TCS has become a large global firm with over 436,000 experienced and well-trained consultants across 46 countries.With a global network delivery model, TCS offers management services, e-business, architecture and technology consulting, engineering, application development and maintenance, security, and infrastructure development to organizations in over 53 countries worldwide. The primary specialization of the firm, and why many leading businesses call on its expertise, is for customized software packages. TCS serves a variety of industries with services including finance, telecommunication, banking, insurance, retail, and many others. Additionally, TCS belongs to the Tata Group, which is one of India’s largest business conglomerates – made up of over 95 companies across 6 continents.
